Royale513's Mission

At a glance our mission is as follows...

It is the desire of Royale513 to become a safe place for young people to learn communication skills, leadership qualities, and just have a great time being a part of something bigger than themselves. Clans themselves give game like Clash Royale a sense of community, add additional leveling options, and introduce co-operative game types. The one thing they currently lack, in our opinion, is structural tools to aid in clan leadership functions. A member of a clan cannot rely on the game's save data or official website to provide any sort of progression tracking outside of said user'scurrent standings for donations and trophies.

Some of our more specific objectives are listed below:

  • Provide better visibility of clan member's contribution to the community through weekly logging of each user's donations
  • Encourage growth of all clan members through weekly logging of trophy ranking
  • Provide similar experiences for other clans and their leaders through the sharing of our web site's template
